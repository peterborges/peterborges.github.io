Process

Heuristic Evaluation

Used internal design guidelines to audit inconsistencies across account summary pages. Uncovered issues around clarity, hierarchy, and unauthenticated access.

Research & Competitive Analysis

Compared USAA tools to banks and fintechs (Capital One, Simple, etc.) and built an internal research library to close gaps in competitor understanding.

Information Architecture

Ran IA tests using Treejack to restructure content based on real user behaviors. Simplified and consolidated account hierarchy across savings, checking, and credit products.

Prototyping & Testing

Developed prototypes in InVision and tested with users via usertesting.com. Iterated based on feedback and performance analytics.

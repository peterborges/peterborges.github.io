Auto AR Pilot

Augmented reality for automotive insight

Timeline: Ongoing from Feb 2018

Roles: Strategy, Wireframing, Visual Design, Experience Design

Overview

An augmented reality platform that lets USAA members explore, identify, and get real-time vehicle information by simply pointing their phones at nearby cars.

Design Drivers

  • 📱 Use AR to simplify car buying research
  • 🧠 Offer insights in a non-intrusive, intuitive way
  • 💡 Empower confident financial decisions with in-context info

How It Works

The user walks around a vehicle while the phone scans and recognizes it. The app returns specs, pricing, ratings, and offers insurance/loan quotes from USAA — all in real time.

User Insights

  • Members want everything integrated and helpful
  • Users lack confidence in major financial decisions
  • Desire for automated, relevant, non-cluttered experiences

Functionality Prioritization

Research led to a leaner MVP — showing relevant info first, with deeper layers accessible through interaction rather than overload.

Testing

Tested with 12 members. Findings confirmed excitement about AR, but highlighted the need for simplified UIs and optional onboarding for first-time users.

Results

  • 📈 810 members registered
  • 🕒 5m 55s avg session time
  • 🔁 44% reuse rate
  • 🚘 Ford F-150 most searched vehicle

Lessons Learned

  • AR display fatigue is real — ergonomic design matters
  • Educating users is essential for emerging tech
  • Not all info belongs in AR — progressive disclosure works better
